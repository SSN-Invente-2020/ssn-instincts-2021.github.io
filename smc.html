SSN MUSIC CLUB

1. Band Hunt


Band Hunt is an online band competition which gives your band a chance to showcase your amazing
musical talent and skills to everyone. Come battle it out with other amazing bands and prove that you have what it takes to be the best.



Rules:


1. This event will be conducted in two stages.
2. Number of members in a band: 3-8.
3. Each member must have registered in the general registration.
4. The video shouldn't exceed 10 mins
5. Shortlisted bands will be intimated via email.
6. The same video will be used for the 2nd round of the event.
7. Original compositions will be awarded extra points.
8. The judge’s decision is final and adhering and in case of any discrepancies, the decision of the coordinators will be binding and final.

For detailed rules and regulations check the brochure.

2. SSN Idol


Looking to prove your mettle as a singer? You’re at the right place. Flaunt your singing skills in the light music solo vocal event. Enthral us with your voice, all eyes are going to be on you
and the stage is all yours. The event is not to be missed for all you aspiring singers!



Rules:


1. This is a two stage event.
2. Participant must get their entry pass and upload it in the registration link
3. Songs performed by participants must be in Indian languages. Presence of a considerable amount of foreign language in a performance might lead to disqualification and this is up to the judge.
4. Participants have to submit a link of their performance(YouTube or Google Drive) not exceeding 5 minutes.
5. Use of any kind of special effects on audio is not allowed.
6. Backing track, shruthi box or one instrumental accompaniment maybe used.
7. The same video will be used for the 2nd round of the event.
8. The judge’s decision will be final.
9. Original compositions will be awarded extra points.
