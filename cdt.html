1. Drutha - Solo


Looking for the perfect opportunity to showcase the passionate dancer in you? Here's one for all classical dancers! All you have to do follow the instructions below to get on board for a fun experience.. Exciting cash prizes await your arrival!


Rules:




1. The contest is open to all college students.
2. Duration strictly between 2-5 minutes.
3. The dance should be a classical piece only.
4. Movie songs are permitted.
5. Stage performances are not allowed.
6. Any video that has been taken in the past 6 months can be sent.
7. Your performance will be judged solely based on your Footwork, expressions, energy and presentation.
8. Any traditional attire is permitted. Any vulgarity in costume or Video will lead to disqualification.
9. Judges rule will be final and binding.
10. Last date for sending entry, on or before April 3rd 2021, 8 AM.

Note: Registration fees of Rs. 100 is to be paid to collect Arudhra's entry pass, which is to be submitted along with the google form. Once you obtain the pass, you will be eligible to apply for both the events - Drutha, Classical Dance Solo and Tattva, Classical Dance Group. Please check Instincts website to get your passes.

2. Tattva - Group



Is your group a bunch of classical dance enthusiasts? Grab on to this chance to bring the best classical performance out of your squad! 
Follow these steps to hop on an amazing ride with us.


Rules:




1. The contest is open to all college students. Inter college teams are welcome.
2. All participants are required to register and collect the event pass.
3. A group can consist of 4-8 members.
4. Duration strictly between 2-5 minutes.
5. The dance should be a classical or semi-classical piece only.
6. Movie songs are permitted.
7. Stage performances are not allowed.
8. Any video that has been taken in the past 6 months can be sent.
9. Your performance will be judged solely based on your Choreography, Concept, Expressions, Energy, Coordination and Overall Presentation.
10. Any traditional attire is permitted. Any vulgarity in costume or Video will lead to disqualification.
11. Usage of props are permitted but not mandatory.
12. Judges rule will be final and binding.
13. Last date for sending entry, on or before April 3rd 2021, 8 AM.

Note: Registration fees of Rs. 100 is to be paid to collect Arudhra's entry pass, which is to be submitted along with the google form. Once you obtain the pass, you will be eligible to apply for both the events - Drutha, Classical Dance Solo and Tattva, Classical Dance Group. Please check Instincts website to get your passes.
